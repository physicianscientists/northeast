About

Boston University School of Medicine and APSA are co-sponsoring an "Exploring Careers as a Physician Scientist" Symposium on December 1, 2018!

Registration is open to anyone with an interest in medicine, research, and a career as a physician scientist. The conference welcomes all trainees in MD/PhD, DO/PhD, MD, DO, health sciences graduate programs, as well as undergraduate students interested in learning more about medical careers and research.

Registration is open to all interested participants at all institutions regardless of APSA membership. Click HERE to register. Registration for the conference is FREE for all attendees.
